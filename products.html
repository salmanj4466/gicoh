GERP

GERP (Gicoh Enterprise Resource Planning System) is our online ERP solution specifically designed for FMCGs . It includes modules like Accounting, Buying, Selling, Stock, Manufacturing, Human Resource and CRM etc and can be integrated with GRMS, which is our retail management system. Fields that you create in it, become instantly available as an API point in the interface of the ERP.
