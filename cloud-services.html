Services

What We Do

Software-As-A-Service (SAAS)

SaaS allows your business to get up and running with a desired software over the internet at minimal upfront cost on pay-as-you-go basis and with appropriate service agreement. The ease of use and subscription based pricing make SaaS one of the most attractive cloud services. SaaS Examples: ERP, CRM, Secondary Sales Software etc.

Platform As A Service (PAAS)

With PaaS, you manage the applications you develop only, and we manage the development/ deployment environment for you, which includes infrastructure, servers, storage, and networking, middleware, development tools, business intelligence (BI) services, database management systems, and more.

Infrastructure-As-A-Service (IAAS)

IaaS includes a virtualised computing infrastructure over the Internet. IaaS scales up and down with demand. You pay for what you use. It helps you avoid the complexities of physical servers and data center infrastructure.

Desktop as a Service (DAAS)

With DaaS we offer virtual desktops to businesses over the Internet licensed on per-user subscriptions. These desktop environments include operating systems and software options as well as storing and saving data for each session. As DaaS data is kept on the server, that makes the updated data available to employees as per their requirements.

Storage as a service (SAAS)

STaaS is a data storage service offered through a subscription package. Enterprises may use it for the storage of their large data, backup and disaster recovery purposes. STaaS Examples: Object Storage, Virtual Machine Disk Storage, File Storage etc.

VOIP and Call Centre Solution

By hosting your Cloud-Based VoIP Call Center Solution with Gicoh Cloud, you may handle high call volumes with fewer agents. Typical features of such a solution could be Interactive Voice Response, Call Recording, Automatic Call Distribution, Call Routing, Dashboard and Reporting etc.
