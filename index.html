Our Products

GERP

Enterprise Resource Planning

GERP is our online ERP solution. It includes modules like Accounting, Procurement, Selling, Stock, Manufacturing, Human Resource, CRM etc and can be integrated with RMS / POS

Learn More

GSSM

Secondary Sales Management

An application that helps you control financials, staff operations, live tracking (sales staff delivery staff), order management (from distributor to company and from shop to distribution) and distributors performance.

GRMS

Retail Management System
